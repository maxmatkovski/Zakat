## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is my data private?**  
Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

**How do I delete a donation?**  
Swipe left on any donation in the list to delete it.

**How do I delete a charity?**  
Go to the Charities tab and swipe left on any entry to delete it.

**Can I use the app on multiple devices?**  
Currently Tzedaka stores data locally on one device. iCloud sync may be added in a future update.
